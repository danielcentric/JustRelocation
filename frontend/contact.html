Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you help me find housing?

For urgent cases, we can arrange temporary accommodation within 24-48 hours and help you find permanent housing within 1-2 weeks. Our emergency housing network is specifically designed for healthcare workers starting NHS positions.

Do you charge fees to healthcare workers?

No, our services are completely free for healthcare workers. We receive commissions from our partner service providers, ensuring you get expert support without any cost to you.

Can you help with NMC/GMC registration?

Yes, we have specialist advisors who assist with professional body registrations. We can help with document preparation, application submission, and liaison with regulatory bodies to expedite the process.

Is support available in other languages?

Yes, we provide support in Tagalog, Hindi, Arabic, French, and several other languages. Our multilingual team includes native speakers from major NHS recruitment countries.

What if I need help outside office hours?

Our emergency support line operates 24/7. For non-urgent matters, you can reach us via WhatsApp or email anytime, and we'll respond within a few hours.

Do you help with family relocations?

Absolutely. We assist with school placements, family housing, visa applications for dependents, and connecting families with local community services and cultural groups.
