Supporting NHS Heroes

We understand the challenges healthcare workers face when relocating to serve the NHS. Our platform simplifies the entire process, connecting you with trusted services and local expertise.

Our Story

JustRelocation was born from a simple observation: healthcare workers coming to serve the NHS face unnecessary bureaucratic hurdles that delay their ability to help patients. Whether you're a Filipino nurse relocating to Manchester, a Nigerian doctor starting in London, or a British healthcare professional moving between trusts, the paperwork and logistics shouldn't be your primary concern.

Founded by professionals who understand both the healthcare sector and the complexities of UK bureaucracy, we've created a comprehensive platform that addresses every aspect of your relocation journey. From finding housing near your assigned hospital to opening bank accounts and completing professional registrations, we've streamlined what used to be a months-long ordeal into a matter of days.

Our research with over 2,000 international healthcare workers revealed that 73% experienced significant delays in their role commencement due to administrative challenges. These delays not only impact the workers themselves but ultimately affect patient care across the NHS. JustRelocation exists to eliminate these unnecessary barriers.

Our Mission

"To ensure that every healthcare worker can begin serving NHS patients within their first week of arrival in the UK, with all administrative and logistical barriers removed."

What We Do

Housing Solutions: We aggregate verified housing providers and landlords who understand NHS shift patterns and offer flexible, affordable housing options within reasonable commuting distance of major hospitals.

Financial Services: We connect you with banking partners who offer streamlined account opening for international healthcare workers, including specialized support for those without UK credit history.

Professional Registration: We connect you with professional registration services that assist with NMC, GMC, and other professional body applications, helping streamline the process for international qualifications.

Family Support: For healthcare workers relocating with families, we offer school placement assistance, childcare options, and family integration services to ensure your loved ones settle successfully.

Transport & Logistics: From airport transfers to car purchases, insurance, and public transport optimization, we handle the practical elements that enable you to focus on patient care.

Why Healthcare Workers Choose JustRelocation

NHS-Specific Expertise: We understand NHS employment structures, pay scales, pension schemes, and the unique challenges of shift work better than generic relocation services.

Verified Partner Network: Every service provider on our platform is vetted, verified, and selected specifically for their experience with healthcare worker relocations. We don't just list services; we curate solutions.

Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees or surprise charges. Our commission-based model means we only succeed when you do, and all costs are clearly disclosed upfront.

24/7 Support: Healthcare doesn't follow office hours, and neither do we. Our support team understands emergency situations and provides assistance when you need it most.

Cultural Sensitivity: Our team includes representatives from the communities we serve, ensuring culturally sensitive support for dietary requirements, religious considerations, and community connections.

Our Partnerships

JustRelocation works in collaboration with NHS England, major teaching hospitals, professional regulatory bodies, and international healthcare worker organizations. We maintain formal partnerships with recruitment agencies across the Philippines, Nigeria, India, and other key NHS recruitment markets.

Our technology integrates with NHS systems where possible, enabling seamless information sharing and reducing duplicate processes. We're committed to supporting the NHS's workforce development goals while ensuring individual healthcare workers receive the support they deserve.
