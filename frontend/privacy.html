Privacy Policy

Your data protection and privacy rights are fundamental to how we operate JustRelocation

GDPR Compliant
Last Updated: 16 September 2024 | Effective Date: 16 September 2024

1. Introduction

JustRelocation Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform and services.

This policy applies to all users of our platform, including NHS healthcare workers seeking relocation services, service providers, and visitors to our website.

Our Commitment

As a platform specifically designed for NHS healthcare workers, we understand the sensitive nature of healthcare-related information and maintain the highest standards of data protection in compliance with UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways when you use our services:

Personal Information You Provide

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Professional information (role, NHS trust, registration numbers)
  • Identity verification documents (passport, visa, work permits)
  • Financial information (for payment processing and credit checks)
  • Housing preferences and requirements
  • Emergency contact information
  • Communication preferences

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, clickstream data)
  • Location data (approximate location based on IP address)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies data

Information from Third Parties

  • Professional verification from regulatory bodies (NMC, GMC)
  • Reference checks from previous employers or landlords
  • Credit checks and financial verification
  • Information from our partner service providers
  • Social media information (only if you choose to link accounts)

Special Categories of Personal Data

We may process special categories of personal data only where necessary and with appropriate safeguards:

  • Health information (only for accommodation needs, e.g., accessibility requirements)
  • Criminal convictions (only for DBS checks required by employers)
  • Racial or ethnic origin (only for cultural/community matching services, if requested)

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information based on the following lawful bases under UK GDPR:

Purpose Legal Basis Data Categories
Platform registration and account management Contract performance Contact details, professional information
Matching with service providers Contract performance Location, preferences, professional details
Payment processing Contract performance Financial information, transaction data
Customer support Contract performance Contact details, communication history

Marketing and Communications

We will only send you marketing communications if you have explicitly consented. You can withdraw consent at any time by:

  • Clicking unsubscribe in any email
  • Updating your preferences in your account settings
  • Contacting us at privacy@justrelocation.com

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information only as necessary to provide our services and as permitted by law:

Service Providers and Partners

  • Housing Providers: Contact details and housing requirements
  • Financial Services Partners: Financial information for account opening
  • Professional Registration Services: Qualification and identity information
  • Transport Providers: Location and travel requirements
  • Educational Institutions: Family information for school placements

Technology and Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting providers (within UK/EU)
  • Payment processors (Stripe, with appropriate safeguards)
  • Communication platforms (WhatsApp Business, with encryption)
  • Analytics providers (anonymized data only)
  • Security and fraud prevention services

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protect our legal rights or the safety of users
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
  • Cooperate with law enforcement investigations

International Transfers

We primarily process data within the UK/EU. Any international transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions. We will inform you of any such transfers and the safeguards in place.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Technical Safeguards

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication for admin access
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with UK/EU data residency

Organizational Safeguards

  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular staff training on data protection
  • Data processing agreements with all third parties
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures
  • Regular audits and compliance reviews

Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify the ICO within 72 hours if required
  • Inform affected individuals without undue delay
  • Take immediate steps to contain and remedy the breach
  • Conduct a full investigation and implement preventive measures

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

Data Type Retention Period Justification
Account information Active account + 2 years Service provision and legal compliance
Transaction records 7 years Financial regulations and tax requirements
Communication records 3 years Customer service and dispute resolution

After the retention period expires, we securely delete or anonymize personal information unless required to retain it by law.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right of Access (Article 15)

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, including details about how it's processed.

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

Request deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances, such as when it's no longer necessary for the original purpose.

Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

Request that we limit how we use your personal information in certain circumstances.

Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

Receive your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format or have it transferred to another controller.

Right to Object (Article 21)

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.

Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)

Not be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling, that produce legal effects.

Exercising Your Rights

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 without parental consent.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 16, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

For family services (school enrollment), we only process children's information with explicit parental consent and for the specific purpose of service provision.
